Tragic Origins

CatNap was not always a monster. Behind this purple, gas-releasing creature is a heartbreaking human story. He was once a young boy named Theodore Grambell, one of the many children subjected to cruel experiments inside the toy factory. These experiments aimed to turn humans into living toys, stripping away their freedom and identity. Theodore's transformation into CatNap erased his childhood, replacing it with obedience and fear.
